Aims and Objectives

  • Mission
    “To produce disciplined students in a perfect learning environment, who will achieve excellence in all human endeavours”.
  • Vision
    “To give a child/student the greatest gift, the root of responsibilities and vein of independence”.
  • First aim
    To cater primarily for the discipline. Academic and moral needs of children of military personnel and ensure continuity of their education.
  • Second aim
    To compliments the efforts of both state and federal government in bringing Post-Primary Education to all.
  • Third aim
    To serve as model secondary school to all existing ones because of the inherent discipline in the military.
